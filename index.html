Origami Instructions

The Chameleon

  1. Chameleons can change color to hide themselves from predators.
  2. The Majority of Chameleons live in Madagascar.
  3. Chameleons can't regrow their tails like other lizards.

The Cicada

  1. Broods (groups) of cicadas come out from their hibernation on a 13 year or 17 year cycle
  2. Cicadas have some of the longest lives for insects because of them coming out as a brood on such a long cycle.
  3. Groups of cicadas can buzz extremely loudly, even louder than things like drills or lawnmowers.
